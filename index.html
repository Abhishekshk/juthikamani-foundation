Quick Overview

Juthikamani Foundation is a private non-profit organization incorporated on October 28, 2020, in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. We work towards fostering social and economic development while embracing India's diverse cultural, linguistic, and religious heritage.

About Juthikamani Foundation

Juthikamani Foundation is a private non-profit organization that stands as a beacon of hope for unity in diversity. Our foundation operates with a deep commitment to the principle that diversity strengthens rather than weakens social fabric.

Our Philosophy

The Juthikamani Foundation's work reflects a deep commitment to the ancient Indian philosophy that views unity in diversity not as mere tolerance of differences, but as a complex understanding that differences enrich human interactions.

Mission & Core Activities

Our Mission

To cultivate novel and unique ideas that promote the cause of unity in diversity, fostering social and economic development while embracing India's diverse cultural, linguistic, and religious heritage.

Socio-Cultural Documentation and Education

We work towards socio-cultural documentation and education, preserving and promoting India's diverse cultural heritage. This aligns with our core mission of unity in diversity.

Community Development Projects

We have undertaken significant community development initiatives, particularly in rural areas. A notable project involved construction assistance for Mala's family in Dumri village, Bihar.

Project Highlight: Completed house construction for a family of eight in Bihar

Conflict Resolution and Legal Education

We have developed expertise in conflict resolution, launching the "Beyond Disputes: A Guide to Conflict Resolution" initiative providing tools and strategies for dispute resolution.
